Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2025

Yellow Bird is a simple touch-based Android game where you control a bird by tapping on the screen. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

This game does not collect or store any personal data.

There is no account login or registration required to play Yellow Bird.

Leaderboard

Yellow Bird includes a leaderboard to display high scores. This feature does not require users to log in, and it does not collect any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Yellow Bird does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.
